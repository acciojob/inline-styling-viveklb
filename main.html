Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive, often colorful, structures of flowering plants (angiosperms) that produce seeds via pollination. They typically feature petals, sepals, and reproductive parts (stamens/pistils) on a stem. Beyond reproduction, flowers are vital for ecosystems, provide food for pollinators, and are used for decoration, medicine, and scent.

Roses pictures

Roses are woody, perennial flowering plants of the genus Rosa (family Rosaceae), comprising over 300 species and thousands of cultivars. Known for their fragrant, showy flowers in various colors, they are native to the Northern Hemisphere. Roses are widely cultivated for beauty, fragrance, food (rose hips), and cultural significance.
